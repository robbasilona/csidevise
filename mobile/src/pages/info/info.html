About Us

RefuGeo is a location-based application that aims to promote disaster preparedness and public safety by incorporating the following features:

  • A resource checklist that allows users to track if they have the necessary materials in their emergency kits such as biscuits, water, medicines, and other necessities that should be procured in preparation for disasters.
  • A dynamic resource locator and navigator, that displays the nearest possible sources of goods such as stores, supermarkets, and groceries that will help users procude materials effectively by adjusting the map depending on the store's supply state and user necessity.
  • An evacuation guide that allows users to see the nearby evacuation areas, as well as an option to navigate to the said evacuation areas. Furthermore, the guide provides a recommendation on which evacuation area each user could go to depending on their location and the occupancy rate of the nearby evacuation areas.
  • A graphic representation of data from previous calamities that show records on occupancy record of nearby evacuation areas, abundance and/or lack of resources during calamities in the area, record of previous calamities in the area, and evacuation records for the nearby evacuation areas.
